## CET-4/6 议论文高分模板（机器批改优化版）

### 模板使用说明
- [关键词] = 题目核心词，全文出现 8-12 次
- 每段 5-8 句，总字数 250-280（要求 180 时）
- 标点后必须空格，段落间空行

---

Nowadays, [关键词] has become a topic of widespread concern across the globe. With the rapid advancement of technology/society, [关键词] has permeated virtually every aspect of human life. It is universally acknowledged that [关键词] brings both remarkable advantages and potential drawbacks. Therefore, how to [题目具体要求] has sparked heated debate among the public.

Firstly, the advantages/benefits of [关键词] are indisputable. To begin with, [论点1 + 展开2-3句]. For instance, [具体例子]. Moreover, [补充论据]. In addition, [进一步展开], which has brought unprecedented [improvement/convenience/efficiency] to [某领域].

However, the disadvantages/drawbacks of [关键词] cannot be overlooked. Not only does [问题1], but it also raises serious concerns regarding [问题2]. Given that [原因分析], the potential for [风险] is considerable. Furthermore, [补充问题]. Were adequate safeguards not implemented, the consequences could be detrimental to [受影响对象].

In conclusion, taking all these factors into consideration, we can safely draw the conclusion that [关键词] is a double-edged sword. Only by [措施1] and [措施2] can we harness its full potential while minimizing its risks. It is our collective wisdom and responsibility that will determine whether [关键词] serves as a blessing or a curse for future generations.

---

### 高分句式速查

**开头段万能句：**
- `Nowadays, ... has become a topic of widespread concern.`
- `It is universally acknowledged that...`
- `With the rapid advancement of..., ... has permeated virtually every aspect of...`

**主体段连接：**
- `Firstly / To begin with / Moreover / In addition`
- `However / Nevertheless / On the contrary`
- `Not only does..., but it also...`
- `Given that..., ... is considerable.`

**总结段万能句：**
- `In conclusion, taking all these factors into consideration...`
- `we can safely draw the conclusion that...`
- `Only by... can we...`
- `It is... that will determine whether...`
