## CET-6 图表描述类作文模板（机器批改优化版）

### 模板使用说明
- 按维度归类数据，不要逐行罗列
- 每个数据动词不重复（参见 SKILL.md 数据描述动词表）
- 总字数 250+（要求 180 时）
- 引用具体数字，不要只说 "increased"

---

### 通用模板

[Table/Figure X] presents a comprehensive overview of [主题] across [N] key indicators from [起始年] to [结束年]. It is clearly illustrated that remarkable progress has been achieved in [领域] over this period.

**Firstly**, the most striking change lies in [指标1], which [plummeted/soared] dramatically from [数据A] to [数据B]. Correspondingly, [指标2] witnessed a substantial [surge/decline], [rising/falling] from [数据C] to [数据D] — an increase of approximately [倍数], rendering [对象] considerably more [adj] than ever before.

**Moreover**, significant improvements have been made in [维度2]. [指标3] nearly [doubled/tripled], rising from [数据E] to [数据F], while [指标4] experienced a precipitous decline from [数据G] to [数据H], indicating that [领域] conditions have been dramatically ameliorated.

**Furthermore**, [维度3]-related statistics are equally impressive. [指标5] climbed from [数据I] to [数据J], and [指标6] increased from [数据K] to [数据L]. Notably, [指标7] skyrocketed from [数据M] to [数据N], which demonstrates that [领域] has been unprecedentedly expanded.

**In conclusion**, the data overwhelmingly indicates that [主题] has yielded exceptional results. Not only has [核心指标] been drastically [reduced/improved], but [其他维度] have also been remarkably elevated. It is this holistic approach that has transformed [对象] over the past [时间段].

---

### 数据引用句式速查

**描述上升：**
- `[指标] witnessed a substantial surge, soaring from X to Y.`
- `[指标] climbed dramatically from X to Y — an increase of approximately Z times.`
- `[指标] skyrocketed from X% to Y%, which demonstrates that...`

**描述下降：**
- `[指标] plummeted dramatically from X to a mere Y.`
- `[指标] experienced a precipitous decline from X to Y.`
- `[指标] dropped sharply from X‰ to just Y‰.`

**描述翻倍/对比：**
- `[指标] nearly doubled, rising from X to Y.`
- `An increase of approximately X times, rendering... considerably more...`
- `While [指标A]..., [指标B] witnessed...`

**归纳意义：**
- `The data overwhelmingly indicates that...`
- `It is clearly illustrated that remarkable progress has been achieved in...`
- `Not only has... been drastically reduced, but... have also been remarkably elevated.`
- `It is this holistic approach that has transformed...`
